## Reporting a Vulnerability
**If you believe you've found a new vulnerability:**
1. DO NOT disclose publicly
2. Email details to: `ghostnetsec96@gmail.com`
3. Include: vulnerability description, steps to reproduce, potential impact

**Response Time:** We acknowledge reports within 24 hours and provide updates every 72 hours.

## Responsible Disclosure
API Hole follows a 90-day coordinated disclosure timeline:
- Day 0: Report received
- Day 1-7: Validation and analysis
- Day 8: Vendor notification (if applicable)
- Day 15: Follow-up with vendor
- Day 90: Public disclosure
